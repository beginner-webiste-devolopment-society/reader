The History of Arthurdale

I am going on a field trip. This field trip is about the city of Arthurdale, West Virginia. “The city was made by First Lady Eleanor Roosevelt as a project for the jobless miners and others. It was one of the projects for the “Reedsville project” it is in Preston County. The town is also named after Richard Arthur. The President once visited” the tour guide explained as he showed us the blacksmith shop, the old church, and many other old historic buildings along with some ruins of an old factory that was used during WWII and was destroyed due to it’s collapsing. It dealt with furniture before it moved to the current location of Superior Fibers. The new factory was a shoe, and sink factory and is now a fiber glass filter plant.
